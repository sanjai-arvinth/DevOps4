About Demon Slayer

"Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his younger sister Nezuko is turned into one. The series is known for its beautiful animation, emotional depth, and intense battles.
